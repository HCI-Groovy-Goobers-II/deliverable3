Welcome to ProjectConnect!

For Students

Picking the right collaborators for a project can feel like a shot in the dark, especially if you haven't met many of your peers. You need to know who's responsible, who communicates well, or who can lead effectively. ProjectConnect aims at taking the guesswork out of choosing your teammates by showing you ratings of other students in your college and in your classes.

Ratings are based off of peer evaluations from past projects, so as more projects are completed, you get a better idea of your peers' strengths and weaknesses. All of this makes it easier on you, the student, to find others who can be a good fit on your next team.

  

For Professors

Class project grades often depend in part on peer evaluations. Creating, distributing, and keeping track of all the evaluations your students complete across courses and sections can be a headache. ProjectConnect streamlines the your ability to distribute and track peer evaluations. You can set them to be sent to your students on a per-project basis at a precise time and view who has completed them in each class.

What's more? For class settings where the professor assigns teams, ProjectConnect offers a great way to get insight into which students will work well together, what problems students are having with their peers, and moderate students' evaluations.
