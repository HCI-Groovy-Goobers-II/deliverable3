About Us

Students

Picking the right collaborators for a project can feel like a shot in the dark, especially if you haven't met many of your peers. You need to know who's responsible, who communicates well, or who can lead effectively. ProjectConnect aims at taking the guesswork out of choosing your teammates by showing you ratings of other students in your college and in your classes. By providing an easy way of searching through to find potential teammates, you can take some stress away of finding the perfect team. {% load static %}

  

Professors

For class settings where the professor assigns teams, ProjectConnect offers a great way to get insight into which students will work well together, what problems students are having with their peers, and moderate students' evaluations.
